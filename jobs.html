Data Analyst

59242

$85,000 - $95,000 per year

Description

S.A Coders' Data Analysts systematically collect, process and perform statistical analyses on data sets, including but not limited to: customer, financial and company data.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create, maintain and update databases, ensuring to uphold data integrity and accessibility for all team members.
  • Assist in the collection, cleaning and analysis of data to support investment decisions and operational processes.
  • Collaborate with senior analysts and the investment team to develop data models and dashboards that enhance decision-making.
  • Generate and maintain reports that give insight into investment performance, risk management, and other key metrics.
  • Create visual representations of data findings to make data understandable.
  • Help inform company decisions by transforming data into actionable insights.

Requirements

Essential Preferable
Bachelor's degree in data science, mathematics, statistics, finance or another related field; or equivalent. Familiarity with SQL or other database management systems.
Experience with Microsoft Excel. An understanding of data governance principles.
Strong analytical skills with the ability to interpret complex data sets and communicate findings clearly. Experience with data integration.
Strong communication and interpersonal skills. Eagerness to learn and adapt in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.
The ability to work collaboratively with teams across different departments. At least six months of work experience is preferable, but not required.
Excellent attention to detail, ensuring accuracy in data analytics and reporting.

Benefits

  1. The opportunity to develop your skills in an experienced, fast-paced and high-energy environment.
  2. Leave benefits including up to 8 weeks paid time off and paid parental leave.
  3. An inclusive and collaborative work environment.
  4. Exposure to work within the finance industry, as well as the inner workings of other corporate departments.

Successful applicants will come into the S.A Coders Office, where they will report to Senior Data Analyst Sophie Kroezen or Finance Team Leader Akhila Sripriyadarshini Gollamudi.

Software Developer

31972

$90,000 - $100,000

Description

S.A Coders' Software Developers program, design, build, implement and maintain software used not only within the company, but also for clients outside of the company.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and enhance our current software solutions.
  • Assist in the creation of software designs for clients, working within a team environment to meet client needs.
  • Drive innovation in the world of development.
  • Write clean, maintainable and efficient code using both front-end and back-end frameworks.
  • Ensure web applications are responsive and compatible across all platforms, company-wide.
  • Work with a unique team to understand and deliver client solutions.

Requirements

Essential Preferable
Bachelor's degree in computer science, software development, information technology or equivalent. Experience working with Javascript, HTML and CSS.
Experience with SQL databases. At least six months work experience in a software development or equivalent role is not essential, but preferred.
Excellent communication and teamwork skills, to enhance productivity amongst teams and increase client understanding. Familiarity with agile development practices.
A genuine passion for technology and sharing it with others. Experience with version control and code management platforms such as GitHub.
A proactive mindset that leaves you process-oriented and focussed on problem-solving.
Flexibility and the ability to take on feedback, but also bounce back from it.

Benefits

  1. A close-knit and actively social team that are inclusive and centered on a diverse working culture.
  2. Attractive time-off benefits, including paid parental leave and up to 8 weeks paid time off per year.
  3. Career development opportunities through working with a diverse range of clients in an ever-expanding company.
  4. Possibility to interact with and gain experience in different corporate fields thanks to cross-department interaction.

Successful applicants will report to Senior Software Developer Charli Rose or Client Satisfaction Team Lead Freddie Mercury.

IT Suppport Technician

60505

$70,000 - $80,000

Description

S.A Coders' IT Support Technicians diagnose, repair and maintain hardware and software components to ensure our computer systems run smoothly.

Key Responsibilities

  • General and basic support with informationa and communication technology.
  • Troubleshooting and diagnosing technical issues both in person and over the phone or online.
  • Perform the provision and installation of IT equipment.
  • Assist in the training and education of employees in new IT equipment and technology.
  • Resolve company hardware issues with laptops, printers, scanners, network equipment and workstations.
  • Ensure compliance with IT security and data protection policies.

Requirements

Essential Preferable
Certificate IV in Computer Systems or Information Technology, or a Bachelor's Degree in a related degree. At least six months of practical work experience in an IT team.
Knowledge and experience with Microsoft applications. Exceptional organisational skills, with a high attention to detail.
The ability to recognise, understand and solve IT issues. Great communication skills, as well as being able to effectively give support online and in person.
Extensive knowledge of IT systems, devices, equipment and related technologies. A positive and collaborative mindset.
The ability to work both alone and within a team environment to solve problems. A passion to learn and continually understand new and evolving technologies.
Understanding and knowledge of data security, integrity and ethics policies.

Benefits

  1. A close-knit and actively social team that are inclusive and centered on a diverse working culture.
  2. Attractive time-off benefits, including paid parental leave and up to 8 weeks paid time off per year.
  3. Career development opportunities through working with a diverse range of technologies and issues in an ever-expanding company.

Successful applicants will report to Senior IT Technician Greg Heffley.
